Who may become a sworn translator?

You may become a sworn translator if you:

  • are a Polish citizen, a citizen of a EU member state, a citizen of an EFTA member state, a citizen of the Swiss Confederation or a citizen of another state if you have a right to be employed or self-employed in Poland,
  • speak and write in Polish;
  • have a right to make legal decisions on your own (full legal capacity);
  • have no criminal record;
  • have a university degree;
  • pass an exam for sworn translators.
Note: In exceptional cases, you may be exempt from an exam if you speak a rare foreign language and there are not enough or no translators of that language in Poland.
