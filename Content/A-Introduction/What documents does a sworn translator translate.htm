What documents does a sworn
translator translate?

A sworn translator may translate a variety of documents from different areas, mainly legal, court and financial documents. Here is a list of the most common documents needed to be translated by a sworn translator.

  • Birth, marriage or death certificates
  • Certificates of no impediment
  • Consent letters for child traveling abroad
  • Certificates of no criminal record
  • School reports, certificates and higher education diplomas
  • Identity documents, driving licenses
  • Car registration documents
  • Bank statements
  • Invoices
  • Articles of Association, company deeds, resolutions
  • Certificates issued by a Tax Office, Registry Office and other authorities
  • Medical certificates
  • Court documents such as judgments, statements of claim
  • Sale agreements, employment agreements
  • Notarial deeds
  • European Arrest Warrant
  • Financial statements
