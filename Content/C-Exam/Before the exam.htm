Before the exam

If you want to take an exam for sworn translators, go to the Ministry of Justice Website, fill in the form “Wniosek o wyznaczenie terminu egzaminu” (Request for an exam date) and send it to the address of the Ministry of Justice given in the form. When you receive information about the date of the exam, you have 7 days to pay an examination fee of PLN 800 to the bank account of the Ministry of Justice.
